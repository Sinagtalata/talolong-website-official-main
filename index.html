Barangay Captain's Message

Thank you for visiting the official website of Barangay Talolong, Municipality of Lopez, Province of Quezon. It's great pleasure that I welcome you all to our Barangay.
As the Barangay Captain of this culturally rich and eco-friendly barangay, it is my wish to bring our barangay closer to the people by providing the necessary information and vision-mission of our barangay.
It is my desire that as you browse through our homepage, news update and department directory, my beloved constituents will have the chance to access the information necessary for your relentless participation in the affairs of Barangay Talolong.
To our visitors, it is my desire that as you click in the link, new stories and gallery photos, you will be amazed that there is more to barangay talolong than just an eco-friendly barangay or the site of the historical Tibag Cave. May this virtual journey encourage you to visit our barangay.
With our motto "Dito sa Talolong, Tayo ay Tulong-Tulong". Problema'y gagaan at maiibsan kapag pinagtulong-tulungang solusyonan. It is my hope that one day, Barangay Talolong wil be known as the "Most inhabitable or livable barangay in our country" All of this can be achieved though tranparency, accountability, responsiveness and our strong faith in God.
"In unity there strength" and with this being united make us stronger. As the bible says in Romans 8:28, "And we know that all things work together for good to those who love God, to those who are called according to His purpose". It means that when we live and work together as the kind of team God intended, Unity results and brings Glory to God because "Teamwork glorifies God"
May God almighty empower us to live out our vision of victory for Barangay Talolong.
"Thank You. TO GOD BE ALL THE GLORY".

Mission and Vision

Misyon ng Barangay

Maiangat ang antas ng pamumuhay ng mga mamamayan at mapanatili
ang isang mapayapang pamayanan.

Pananaw ng Barangay

Isang pamayanang nagkakaisa, sumusunod sa mga alituntuning pinaiiral ng mga nakatataas at sama-sama sa pagtuklas ng mga positibo at alternatibong paraan para sa ika-uunlad ng barangay. Mas maganda, mapayapa at nagkakaisang pamayanan tungo sa ika-uunlad at may dekalidad na pamumuhay.

Talolong Municipality of Lopez Quezon

Talolong, formerly Poblacion, is a barangay in the municipality of Lopez, in the province of Quezon. Its population
as determined by the 2021 Census was 4,627 This represented the total population of Lopez.

Tibag Cave

A man-made cave shelter dating back the 2nd World War. Said to be a hiding place of the guerrillas who fought the Japanese invades. Located in Barangay Talolong.

Location & Geography

Talolong is situated at approximately 13.8820, 122.2621, in the island of Luzon. Elevation at these coordinates is
estimated at 8.5 meters or 27.9 feet above mean sea level.
