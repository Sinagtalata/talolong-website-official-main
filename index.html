Mission and Vision

Misyon ng Barangay

Maiangat ang antas ng pamumuhay ng mga mamamayan at mapanatili
ang isang mapayapang pamayanan.

Pananaw ng Barangay

Isang pamayanang nagkakaisa, sumusunod sa mga alituntuning pinaiiral ng mga nakatataas at sama-sama sa pagtuklas ng mga positibo at alternatibong paraan para sa ika-uunlad ng barangay. Mas maganda, mapayapa at nagkakaisang pamayanan tungo sa ika-uunlad at may dekalidad na pamumuhay.

Tibag Cave

A man-made cave shelter dating back the 2nd World War. Said to be a hiding place of the guerrillas who fought the Japanese invades. Located in Barangay Talolong.

Talolong Municipality of Lopez Quezon

Talolong, formerly Poblacion, is a barangay in the municipality of Lopez, in the province of Quezon. Its population
as determined by the 2021 Census was 4,627 This represented the total population of Lopez.

Location & Geography

Talolong is situated at approximately 13.8820, 122.2621, in the island of Luzon. Elevation at these coordinates is
estimated at 8.5 meters or 27.9 feet above mean sea level.
